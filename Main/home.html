Our Story

At Viking, we're passionate about helping athletes of all levels achieve their goals. We believe that the right pair of shoes can make all the difference when it comes to performance, comfort, and injury prevention. That's why we're committed to offering the best selection of sports shoes on the market. Our journey began in Sydney, where we opened our first store in 2005. Since then, we've grown to become a trusted name in the local sports community. Our team of experts is dedicated to providing personalized service and helping our customers find the perfect shoes for their needs.

Our Values

  • Quality: We believe in offering only the highest quality products.
  • Expertise: Our team of knowledgeable staff are here to help you find the perfect shoes for your needs.
  • Community: We're committed to building strong relationships with our customers and supporting the local sports community.
  • Innovation: We stay up-to-date with the latest developments in sports shoe technology and design to bring you the best products on the market.
